Advancements in Face Research: A Conversation Between Humans and Machines

Patrik Huber, Paul Koppen, Zhenhua Feng | 23 Jun 2016

The event will cover new and emerging techniques in the area of face research and analysis, providing a means of discussing your research to get feedback from similar fields. This also provides you with an opportunity to brain storm ideas on how to further the current state of the art.
